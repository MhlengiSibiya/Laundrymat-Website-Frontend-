Clothing

Our professional clothing service includes both a comprehensive wash, dry & iron option and an iron only option. For wash, dry & iron service, pricing is R65 for small and medium items and Large R85 for larger garments, while iron only is R45. We ensure each garment is treated with care using hypoallergenic detergents and temperature-controlled processes.

⏱️ Average Time: 4-6 hours

Bedding

Expert cleaning for your bedding items including blankets and comforters. Single blankets/comforters are cleaned for R65, while double, queen, and king sizes are each available for R95. We use gentle cleaning solutions to maintain fabric quality and ensure fresh, hygienic bedding.

⏱️ Average Time: 48 hours

Curtains

Professional cleaning of curtains to keep them bright and dust-free. Our process ensures that all types of curtains are handled with care and cleaned thoroughly for only R95. Enjoy the benefits of our meticulous treatment that preserves the fabric and color integrity.

⏱️ Wash Cycle: 30 mins ⏱️ Dry Cycle: 45 mins
